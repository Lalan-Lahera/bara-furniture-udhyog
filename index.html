About Us / हाम्रो बारेमा

Bara Furniture Udhyog is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of high-quality furniture and home goods.

English

We manufacture premium furniture for homes, offices, hotels, and institutions. We also stock a wide range of home goods including chairs, baby chairs, carpets, doormats, and steel cupboards (daraz). We serve both retail and wholesale buyers across Nepal.

नेपाली

हामी घर, अफिस, होटल र संस्थानका लागि उच्च गुणस्तरका फर्निचर निर्माण गर्छौं। हामी कुर्सी, बेबी चेयर, कार्पेट, डोरम्याट, र स्टिल दराज लगायतका गृह सामाग्री पनि बिक्री गर्छौं। खुद्रा तथा थोक दुवै सेवा उपलब्ध छ।
